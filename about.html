Who is Wing C

My name is Wing Chan, and I am a third-year Fashion Design student at ASU FIDM dedicated to refining my technical abilities and developing a thoughtful, modern design perspective. Growing up with both Chinese and Western cultural influences, I learned to appreciate diverse aesthetics, traditions, and forms of craftsmanship. This blend of perspectives informs my design approach and helps me create garments that balance cultural sensitivity with contemporary style.

Throughout my academic journey, I have focused on garment construction, pattern drafting, textiles, and digital design, which allows me to approach each project with both creativity and technical precision. I am particularly interested in creating pieces that combine modern aesthetics with functional, intentional design.

As I continue to advance in my program, I remain committed to strengthening my craftsmanship, expanding my portfolio, and preparing for a professional career in the fashion industry.

Thank you for taking the time to view my work.
